About

Archtalk is a revolutionary AI-powered platform that transforms architectural modeling through natural language interaction. Our technology enables architects, designers, and builders to create, modify, and visualize 3D models using simple conversational commands, making the design process more intuitive and accessible.

Lead Manager & Full-Stack Developer

John Xianfeng Jiang

Columbia University GSAPP

John is a New York/ Tokyo-based architect, AI researcher, and project manager. He has over seven years of professional experience at Kengo Kuma & Associates (KKAA) and is a member of the Architectural Institute of Japan (AIJ). Currently, he is exploring the development of large language models (LLMs) to contribute to the architecture, engineering, and construction industries.
